Airports

Ireland hosts many airports, such as:

  • Dublin Airport
  • Knock Airport
  • Cork Airport
  • Kerry Airport

With this wide variety of airports in Ireland, tourists will never be any more than a 5 hour drive from any destination in Ireland.

Trains

Ireland has many train stations dotted throughout the country. Most notably in Dublin, Galway, Westport, Athlone, Cork, Limerick.

Trains run frequently throughout the day and makes the country easy to traverse.

Our Train network means that you are no longer than a 5 hour train journey from Dublin.

Ireland's own train network, Irish Rail, run trains throughout the country multiple times a day.

In Dublin, there are more trains available such as the DART and LUAS, which help you navigate the city.

Busses

Ireland has many bus providers, such as Bus Eireann, Expressway, and CityLink.

Bus Eireann is the national bus service and provides routes both locally and nationally.

CityLink offers direct routes from cities in Ireland, such as the Galway-Dublin route.

There are many local bus providers in more rural areas also to help you commute.

We offer Leapcards to help commuters pay their fares for a reduced rate.
